Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2025

Welcome to Module Tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Module Tracker, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address, name (optional), and authentication credentials
  • Academic Data: Semester information, module details, assessments, grades, and study schedules
  • User Preferences: App settings, color preferences, notification preferences, and customization options
  • Notes and Content: Any notes, comments, or content you create within the app

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports, and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Authentication Tokens: Securely stored tokens for maintaining your session

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Module Tracker service
  • Synchronize your data across devices
  • Calculate and display your academic progress and grades
  • Send you notifications about deadlines and assessments (if enabled)
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and improve app stability
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Cloud Storage: Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud Platform) - encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Local Storage: Hive database on your device for offline access
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with industry-standard security

3.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • User-scoped database rules (you can only access your own data)
  • Secure authentication with Firebase Auth
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Module Tracker uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your academic data
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to improve app stability

Learn more: Firebase Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and name from Google. Google's Privacy Policy applies: Google Privacy Policy

4.3 Sign in with Apple

If you sign in with Apple, we receive minimal information as per Apple's privacy-first approach. Apple's Privacy Policy applies: Apple Privacy Policy

5. Your Data Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Stop using the app at any time

To delete your account: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data from our servers.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backup Data: Backups are retained for 90 days for disaster recovery
  • Crash Logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes

7. Children's Privacy

Module Tracker is intended for university students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The mobile app does not use cookies. The web version may use session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We use Google Cloud Platform's Firebase services, which comply with GDPR and international data protection standards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for major changes)

Continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
